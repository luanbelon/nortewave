About Nortewave

The Specialist in Premium Accessories

Nortewave represents premium accessories brands in the world in the Telecom and Consumer Electronics sectors for the Iberian cluster (Portugal, Spain and Andorra).

Since its foundation in 2013, the Porto-based company has quickly become one of the most important and reliable distributors of premium smartphone accessories.

We provide customized business solutions, extensive industry experience, great marketing support, and unconditional service.
