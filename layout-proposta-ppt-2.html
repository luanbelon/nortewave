About Nortewave

The Specialist in Premium Accessories

Nortewave represents premium accessories brands in the Telecom and Consumer Electronics sectors for the Iberian cluster (Portugal, Spain and Andorra).

Since its foundation in 2013, the Porto-based company has quickly become one of the most important and reliable distributors of premium smartphone accessories.

With simplicity, flexibility and passion, we support our partners with consistency, speed and focus on long-term growth.
