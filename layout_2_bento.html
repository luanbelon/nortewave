The Specialist in Premium Brands.

Nortewave represents premium accessories brands in the world in the Telecom and Consumer Electronics sectors for the Iberian cluster (Portugal, Spain and Andorra).

Since its foundation in 2013, the Porto-based company Nortewave has quickly become one of the most important and reliable distributors of premium smartphone accessories. All our brands are among the market leaders in their segment and are absolute specialists in their field.

With simplicity, flexibility and passion, the company grows exponentially thanks to highly passionate professionals - always full of energy!

Our Core
